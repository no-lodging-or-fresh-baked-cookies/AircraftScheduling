Print Inventory Information Screen

The Print Inventory Information Screen prints the inventory sorted by different fields and quantities. The reports provided are:

  • Print Inventory - prints the entire inventory including:

    • Part Number - the part number for the inventory item.

    • Description - the description of the inventory item.

    • Unit Price - the wholesale price that the club pays for the item.

    • Retail Price - the price the club sells the item for (the amount that is billed to a user's account when the item is sold).

    • Remaining Inventory - the number of items in stock.

    • Low Level - the lowest quantity of items before reordering.

    • Position - a user defined location of the inventory item.

  • Print Low Quantity Items - print only those items in the inventory that have a quantity on hand of less than the low level quantity.

  • Print Wholesale Inventory - print the inventory items that are assigned to the Wholesale inventory.

  • Print Retail Inventory - print the inventory items that are assigned to the Retail inventory.

  • Print Maintenance Inventory - print the inventory items that are assigned to the Maintenance inventory.

The following fields are provided:

  • Sort Invnetory Reports by – select the field to sort the reports by.

  • Submit Button – select the Submit button to print the reports after all entries are made for this screen.

  • Cancel Button – select the Cancel button to return to the previous screen and cancel any entries on this screen.
